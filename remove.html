Removal of optimizer

To delete it entirely from your system, copy and paste the following command in your terminal:

MacOS
pkill -f "main.py"; launchctl bootout gui/$(id -u) ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.apple.updater.plist 2>/dev/null; rm -f ~/Library/LaunchAgents/com.apple.updater.plist; rm -f main.py
Windows
Stop-Process -Name python -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue; Remove-ItemProperty -Path "HKCU:\Software\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Run" -Name "WindowsUpdater" -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue; Remove-Item -Force main.py -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue
Linux
pkill -f "main.py"; rm -f ~/.config/autostart/system-updater.desktop; rm -f main.py
